About me

I caught fire designing.

I always thought my dream was to be a football player in one the Europe's top five leagues; winning trophies and world titles. It seemed quite an appropriate dream given my outstanding athletic qualities. I gravitated towards Graphic Designing in my first year on campus at the University of Ghana. And then that's when it happened, the spark i was missing ignited the instant i clicked play on my very first Adobe XD User Interface design tutorial video on Youtube. Since then, I haven't been able to get a hold off of designing. I incorporate creativity and problem solving techniques to provide users and with the best of User Interface designs. I enjoy meeting new people and hearing new perspectives. Reach out to me if you would like to talk about creating stunning User Interface/User Experiences to help improve your software products. I had my Senior Secondary Education at Mfantsipim School and I am currently pursuing an undergraduate degree in Computer Science at the University of Ghana-Legon.
